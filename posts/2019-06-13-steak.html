Brazilian steakhouse

Posted on June 13, 2019 by Benjamin Goldstein

I was invited to a friend’s birthday dinner at a Brazilian Steakhouse in Sydney. I called them ahead of time, and to my great joy, thanksgiving and surprise, some of their meat was suitable, so I was able to attend. The juciest, most succulent end to the week.
