Homebrew on your XBox

Posted on November 1, 2018 by Benjamin Goldstein

So I know what you are thinking.

If you are looking for some enjoyment, and you have an xbox, why not just play games on it?

Well that is not what true programmers do.

Late on a Thursday night, I managed to get homebrew running on my old XBox! After 5 minutes moving the cursor with the controller, I could no longer stand this and walked the 15 minutes to buy a compatible (USB) keyboard from the local IT shop.

It works!
