My most-forgotten power shell tips of 2018

Posted on July 12, 2018

Finding a file with a name in PowerShell:

Get-ChildItem -Path C:\fanfiction -Filter masterchief.txt -Recurse -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue -Force

Get a quick summary of files in a dir:

$di,$fi = gci -r -force | measure -sum PSIsContainer,Length -ea 0

Show help examples for some object:

Get-Help Some-Object -examples | more

Find which tab is causing your chrome to lag:

Get-Process chrome\* | Select-Object processname,ID,CPU | sort CPU
